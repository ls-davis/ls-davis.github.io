Problem Statement

Due to the University of South Carolina's large student population, many locations -- such as academic, dining, and fitness centers -- often experience overcrowding and long wait times. These issues can place an undo burden upon the busy schedules of students as well as faculty. Our app seeks to provide a lightweight, accurate platform that tracks crowd density and wait times of popular locations in real-time.
